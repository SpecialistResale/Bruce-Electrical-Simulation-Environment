# Engineering Notes

## Modeling assumptions
- Most v1 models are lumped-parameter and quasi-static.
- Geometry is simplified to produce rapid insight.
- Thermal and magnetic nonlinearities are treated as second-order effects unless explicit.

## Recommended extensions
- Coupled thermal-electromagnetic transient models.
- Finite-element field solvers for geometry-heavy layouts.
- Material databases with temperature and frequency dependence.
- Proximity effect corrections and busbar force estimation under fault transients.
